Accountability

One of the major aims of the Oversight website is to improve the transparency on the activities of our elected officials in order to be able to hold them properly accountable for their actions

Democracy

To foster the democratic environment in Nigeria by ensuring that the citizens have reliable and sufficient information about their leaders in order to be able to make more informed decisions.

Leadership

To encourage and foster effective leadership by maintenance of proper monitoring of activities of elected representatives and highlighting those activities to the citizens
