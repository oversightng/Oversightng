About Oversight Nigeria

Oversight is a Nigerian Non-Governmental Organisation established in 2017. The main objective of this organisation is to monitor the activities of the elected representatives of the Nigerian people and to provide the citizens of Nigeria with relevant information in regards to their representatives so that they are better equipped to make informed decisions.

Accountability One of the major aims of the Oversight website is to improve the transparency on the activities of our elected officials in order to be able to hold them properly accountable for their actions

Democracy To foster the democratic environment in Nigeria by ensuring that the citizens have reliable and sufficient information about their leaders in order to be able to make more informed decisions.

Leadership To encourage and foster effective leadership by maintenance of proper monitoring of activities of elected representatives and highlighting those activities to the citizens
